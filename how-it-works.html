How it works

Collective Research runs transparent, collaborative benchmark studies. Here is exactly what that means, how a study runs, what you get in return for your participation, and how your data is treated.

Our vision

An honest picture, for every company

We believe every company deserves a clear, honest look at where it stands in its digital transformation journey, measured against real industry peers, not hunches or generic industry claims.

Our mission

Built together, useful for everyone

Every study works the same way: companies provide a small amount of information about themselves and receive in return a personalized summary of their own results. The more companies participate, the fuller the picture becomes for everyone, including you.

The process

How a study runs, step by step

1. Early access

You sign up to be notified. It's free and commits you to nothing.

2. Launch

Once enough companies have signed up, we announce a launch: how many are already on board and a clear survey window (roughly 30 days).

3. Survey period (roughly 30 days)

You fill out a confidential 10- to 15-minute survey about your company. Right after, you get your personal Digital Maturity Snapshot, a summary of your results.

4. Full report (roughly 45 days post-launch)

After the survey closes, we compile the full, peer-compared report of all participants and make it available to you.

What you get

Free, immediate and growing over time

Your Snapshot

For every participant right after the survey: your own scores in all measured dimensions.

The Benchmark report

A more detailed report comparing you to similar companies by sector and size, made available to participants at a discount when the study ends.

Past studies

As Collective Research runs other studies on different topics, anonymized findings from past studies become available to explore.

A voice on what comes

Participants help shape future surveys, this is version one and feedback really does change what we ask and how we report.

Data and privacy

What we collect and what we don't

We don't need personal information to run these studies and we don't ask for it. Information about your organization and its operations is enough. Your individual answers are never shared with anyone else, not with other participants. Only anonymized, aggregated data is used to build benchmarks and reports.

Methodology

Based on established research, openly improved

Our surveys are based on established, widely-studied frameworks for digital maturity, not made up from scratch. Methodology is reviewed by independent academics and industry consultants, and every study is treated as a version: feedback from participants shapes the next one directly.

Who's involved

Companies, consultants and independent experts

Most participants are companies taking part in a study. Alongside them, academic researchers help review our methodology and independent consultants are sometimes available if a company wants support in an area its results highlight, that's a connection we help make, not the endorsement or certification of any one person or firm.
