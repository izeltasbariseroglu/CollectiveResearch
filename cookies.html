Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us make the website function properly, make it more secure, and understand how the website performs.

2. How we use cookies

Currently, we only use strictly necessary cookies to save your language preference (e.g., local storage). We do not use third-party marketing or tracking cookies.

3. Managing cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features like language switching.
