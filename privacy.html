Privacy policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026, Draft for early access

1. What we collect

We don't need personal information to run these studies and we don't ask for it. The information we collect falls into two categories:

  • Contact data: if you sign up for early access, we store your email address, participant type (company, consultant/advisor or academic/researcher), your organization name, and sector.
  • Survey responses: when a study is live, the answers you provide describe your organization (its tools, processes and practices), not you as an individual.

2. How we use your data

Your own survey answers are used to create your personal Digital Maturity Snapshot and later your benchmark report. Beyond that, your individual answers are combined with all the others, anonymized and aggregated to create the overall benchmark. No individual company answers are ever shown or recognized by anyone else.

3. Who can see your data

  • Your own results are visible only to you.
  • Aggregated, anonymized statistics appear in benchmark reports and may be shared publicly or sold as research findings.
  • We never sell or share your individual answers, contact data, or company-identifiable data with third parties.

4. Where your data is stored

Data is stored on servers located within the European Union. Access is limited to those who operate Collective Research and appropriate technical safeguards are in place.

5. How long we keep it and deletion

Contact data is kept until you ask us to delete it or until the corresponding study ends, whichever comes first. Survey answers are kept as long as they're needed to create your report and contribute to aggregated benchmarks. You can ask us to delete your contact data or revoke your survey answers by emailing privacy@collectiveresearchs.com.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you are, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. Contact privacy@collectiveresearchs.com to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to this policy

As Collective Research evolves, this policy may be updated. We will note the update date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

Any questions about this policy or how your data is treated can be sent to privacy@collectiveresearchs.com.

This page describes our intended data handling in plain language. It is provided for transparency during early access and is not a substitute for formal legal advice.
